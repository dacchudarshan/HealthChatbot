Medical Chatbot with NLP & BERT

An intelligent healthcare assistant powered by advanced natural language processing and deep learning technologies.

About the Project

This healthcare chatbot is designed to provide accurate medical information and preliminary advice regarding bone-related conditions and fractures. Leveraging state-of-the-art NLP techniques and BERT-based models, the system understands complex medical queries and provides contextually relevant responses.

The chatbot has been trained on a vast corpus of medical literature, clinical guidelines, and patient-doctor interactions to ensure the information provided is both accurate and clinically relevant. It serves as a first point of contact for patients seeking information about bone health, symptoms, and treatment options.

Technology Stack

BERT Model

Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers for understanding medical context and generating accurate responses.

NLP Pipeline

Advanced natural language processing for intent recognition, entity extraction, and context understanding.

Medical Knowledge Base

Curated database of ill conditions, symptoms, treatments, and rehabilitation protocols.

How the Chatbot Works

  1. User Input: The patient asks a question about bone health or symptoms
  2. Intent Recognition: NLP identifies the purpose of the question
  3. Context Understanding: BERT model analyzes the context and medical relevance
  4. Knowledge Retrieval: System fetches relevant information from medical databases
  5. Response Generation: The chatbot formulates a human-like, clinically appropriate response

Key Features

Symptom Analysis

Helps users understand potential bone-related conditions based on described symptoms.

Treatment Information

Provides details about common treatments, medications, and rehabilitation exercises.

Emergency Guidance

Identifies potential emergency situations and advises immediate actions.

Doctor Recommendations

Suggests when professional medical consultation is necessary.
